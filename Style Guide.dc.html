07 · Scroll Box & Tint Bands The signature page shell
Division & service pages end in a floating #0A0A0A box that expands from 90% to full-bleed as you scroll into it (shadow fades out), then stays full. Inside, content sections alternate background so they don't blend:
Base band #0A0A0A
Tint band #101010 + inset top highlight
Base band #0A0A0A
Tint band #101010
Faded mini-hero
Base section — lead prose sits on #0A0A0A.
Tint section — #101010 lifts it just enough to separate.
Base section again — photos & CTA split-bands act as natural separators between.
Reveal-on-scroll via [data-reveal] (IntersectionObserver rooted on the box). Service pages + About use this with no hero — straight into the box.
