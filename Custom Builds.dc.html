Ground-up custom builds, from concept to keys.

Red Box Motors builds complete, ground-up custom cars at our Austin, Texas shop, minutes from Circuit of the Americas. From bolt-on aero to full Liberty Walk, Brabus and LART widebody conversions, every kit is fitted and blended into the bodywork, refinished in-house and protected — never simply screwed on and handed back.

Because bodywork, paint, STEK paint protection film, vinyl wraps, wheels, interior, audio and exhaust all happen under one roof, the disciplines are planned together instead of bolted on after the fact. One team scopes the car, sequences the work and owns the result — so a full build looks as intentional as a single detail.
