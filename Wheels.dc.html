Wheels are the first thing curb rash and brake dust ruin — and the quickest transformation on the whole car. We strip each wheel back, repair curb damage, and refinish it in a custom color or powder-coat that's tougher and more even than paint.

— The process

Strip, refinish, balance.

01
Strip & repair

Tires are dismounted and each wheel is stripped to bare metal, then curb rash is filled, lips straightened and faces machined true.

02
Refinish

The prepped wheel is powder-coated or resprayed in your color and finish, then cured hard for a durable, even surface.

03
Mount & balance

Tires are remounted, road-force balanced and the wheels torqued back to spec so the finished set drives out true.
